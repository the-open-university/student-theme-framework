Internet

Save page

The internet is now the most widely used tool used by employers to advertise vacancies and by people looking for work.

Due to the vast number of jobs advertised it’s important that you make your search targeted to avoid being inundated with information about inappropriate vacancies.

Use a variety of recruitment sites to search for jobs and register yourself as looking for work. Registering with job search sites means you’ll receive email alerts about jobs that fit your search criteria. In addition to registering with a range of popular sites such as Monster and TotalJobs make sure you also look directly at the sites of employers you are interested in working for and specific industry sector recruitment sites.
