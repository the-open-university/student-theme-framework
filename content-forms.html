Forms

Here are a couple of form examples. It is more important that the form you create is usable and accessible than that it follows the coding detailed below. Since there can be many different types of forms for all different kinds of usage please feel free to design them with the spirit of OU STF in mind.

If you need a list within a form

Any lists within a form need the class "ou-form-list", like so:

  • Break your topic down into the areas you wish to research
  • Please keep your answer down to under 50 words
  • Are you focussing on a particular angle?
